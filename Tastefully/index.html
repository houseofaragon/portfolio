Tip #1: Don't throw away parsely (or any leftover herbs).

I always seem to have an abundance of left over herbs just sitting in the fridge, getting old and limp. Instead of tossing them in the garbage, use this technique to freeze them in ice trays using butter. In the future, all you have to do is pop them out and toss in a frying pan. Wow, so easy.
